About Story

Company Profile

Tropical Parts & Services (TPS) is the authorized distributor of Global Auto Parts (GAP) products in Ghana. The company deals in most popular automobile brands of Japanese, Korean, European, and American vehicle makes such as Nissan, Toyota, Honda, Mazda, Hyundai, Kia, Suzuki, Opel, Mitsubishi, Volvo, and Ford. We stock a wide range of fast moving, high mortality, service, and repair parts such as Braking , Suspension & Steering, Filtration, Axle & Transmission, Cooling & Heating, Electrical & Lighting, Engine Parts, Lubricants, Fluids & Chemicals, Service Parts, and Body & Exhaust. Providing genuine quality parts with maximum convenience and highly competitive pricing to our customers is our pride, whiles continually searching for ways to expand our product range and improve our service to customers.​

GAP Highest Quality Manufacturing

Global Auto Parts (GAP) differentiates itself by using its substantial experience of automotive manufacturing to identify factories that make parts for major vehicle manufacturers across the world. Global Auto Parts has supplier contracts with many such manufacturers supplying the same quality of parts branded as Global Auto Parts. GAP import high volumes of these parts and break the bulk at their state-of-the-art warehouse and distribution facility in Dubai for distribution to various territories across the globe. GAP parts are a true OE (original equipment) match in terms of design, materials, operation, fit, and durability. This model gives all GAP's customers an opportunity to compete with the vehicle manufacturer branded parts and other known aftermarket brands.
